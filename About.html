Hello guys! Thanks for visiting
my page and actually clicking on this link to
find out more about who I am. First off, my name is Sarah Benson
and I was born in a small town called Salsbury located in North Carolina. I lived and
grew up there until I finished college and then I moved to chase my dreams. After a while, I ended up
finding a place to call home in Mystic Falls, Virginia where I currently reside. I currently work with multiple artists around the world and help us all centralize in a virtual world to share our works and allow others
to enjoy them as well. I have a passion for painting and they are all I collect, help share, and
display. I make some of my own work; however, I mainly help other artists
and share their wonderful feats of paintings. So take your time
on this website and enjoy the paintings!
Thank you!
